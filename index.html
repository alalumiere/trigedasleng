Welcome to Trig!

Trigedasleng (Trig) is a language spoken by the Grounders. Grounders ar epeopel who were born on earth and make up the clans in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States. Trigedasleng was first used by the Trikru clan and means "forest language". Trigedasleng is not a written language.

My goal wiht this app is to use technology to make learning a new language easier.
